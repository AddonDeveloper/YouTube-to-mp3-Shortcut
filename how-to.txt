- Visit YouTube.com and select a Video you want to convert to mp3
- Now go to the adress bar. For Example: https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=0KSOMA3QBU0
- Type rc before "youtube.com/watch?v=" like "rcyoutube.com/watch?v="
- The final url must be https://www.rcyoutube.com/watch?v=0KSOMA3QBU0
- Press enter and you go direct to the file download.
